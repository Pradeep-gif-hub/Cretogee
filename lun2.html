Exploring Space. Shaping Tomorrow.

At CRETOGEE, we are blending Machine Learning and Space Science to predict and visualize lunar and planetary craters, using ISRO's IIRS and NASA's datasets.

Our Projects

ML Model for Crater Prediction

Building deep learning models trained on ISRO's IIRS and NASA datasets to predict crater structures with high accuracy.

AI-based Crater Image Generation

Generating realistic crater visuals using advanced image synthesis and generative AI techniques for research and simulation purposes.

About Us

CRETOGEE is a community of passionate explorers and innovators, working on cutting-edge crater prediction models and AI-driven crater image generation. Our mission: to empower space research through the synergy of machine learning and astronomical data.
